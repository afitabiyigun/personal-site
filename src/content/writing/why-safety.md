We keep building systems that do increasingly consequential things, and the
mapping between what we ask them to do and what they actually do is not yet
clean enough for me to sleep well. Every time we get better at building them,
the mapping matters more. Working on that mapping — making the insides legible,
bounding the failure modes — feels like one of the most useful things a
person with a CS + math + psych background can do right now.
