Humans are not legible to themselves. We infer our own motives after the
fact, often wrongly. The trick we play in psychology — asking a person *why*
they did something and taking the answer at face value — is the same trick we
would play by asking a model to self-report its reasoning.

So "interpretability" may be the wrong frame. We want *legibility under the
specific lens that matters for the specific decision*, not a total readout.
