Writing a confidence level next to a claim is a small act with big effects.
It forces you to feel the difference between "I'm pretty sure" and "I'm
confident enough to bet on this." It also invites the reader to disagree on
*that axis*, not just on the claim itself.

Every note in this garden carries a status chip (seedling / budding /
evergreen) and a confidence slider. You can drag the slider to your own
number — the page remembers it locally.
